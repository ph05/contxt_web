What is Contxt

Your experiences. Your people. Your context.

Contxt is a privacy-first experience-logging app built on one belief: the best recommendations come from the people you trust, not algorithms, not strangers, not influencers.

Log restaurants, movies, trips, albums, and everyday moments. Share a thought. Your circle is intentionally small, so every post lands with people who care. No followers, no public profiles, no viral anything.

Rate & Review

Log experiences across five categories: Media, Places, Eats, Stuff, and Experiences. Star ratings, written reviews, and photos, all in one place.

Your Circle

A small, intentional circle. Bi-directional only, so both people confirm. No follower counts, no clout chasing. Just the people whose opinions you actually value.

Thoughts & Pics

Not everything needs a rating. Drop a quick thought or snap a photo. No category, no stars, just sharing a moment with your circle.

Privacy First

No algorithmic feeds, no data selling, no engagement tricks. Chronological feed. Your data stays yours. We don't show you ads.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Contxt ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Content you create: Posts, reviews, ratings, photos, comments, and messages you share within the app.
  • Friend connections: The list of friends in your circle, stored to deliver your personal feed.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your location in the background.
  • We do not collect contacts from your address book.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not use your data for behavioral advertising or algorithmic profiling.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Contxt experience: displaying your feed, delivering messages, showing friend activity, and diagnosing technical issues. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for authentication, data storage, and hosting. Crash reports are processed through Sentry. We use Mixpanel for anonymous, aggregated usage analytics.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security practices including secure authentication, Firestore security rules, and access controls.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app. Upon account deletion, your posts, profile, friend connections, and messages are permanently removed. Content that other users have already viewed in their feed may remain visible in their local cache temporarily.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, file storage.
  • Sentry: Error monitoring and crash reporting.
  • Mixpanel: Anonymous usage analytics.

Children's Privacy

Contxt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email. Continued use of Contxt after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
